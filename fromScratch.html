Tips for Beginners

  1. Start with the essentials
    • Get a guitar that feels comfortable in your hands
    • Keep a tuner, pick, and capo nearby
    • Practice in a spot where you can stay relaxed
  2. Build the basics first
    • Learn a few easy chords and smooth transitions
    • Use a metronome to improve timing
    • Play slowly before trying to play fast
  3. Make it fun
    • Practice songs you actually like
    • Play with friends whenever you can
    • Stay patient because progress comes with repetition
