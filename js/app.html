Simple Guitar App

Pick chords. Hear them. Build a loop.

Learn to recognize chords by hearing their tones, then build your own 4-chord loop and play it back.

1. Choose a Key

Start by choosing the key you want. The app will show the most common chords in that key.

2. Click a Chord

Each chord button plays matching tones. If you like it, add it to your 4-chord loop.

Now Playing Nothing yet. Click a chord to hear it.

3. Build Your Loop

Your loop can hold 4 chords. Use the buttons below to hear the full progression or a random idea.

How it works The sounds are created in the browser with the Web Audio API. Each chord plays a few notes together so you can hear a simple version of its tone.
